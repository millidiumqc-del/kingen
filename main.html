Welcome to KingGen Key Hub

This is the central hub for accessing the KingGen script. Connect with Discord to manage your key status and gain access.

PERM Key Access:

If your account has **Permanent** status, your key is active forever. Your Roblox User ID will be linked upon first execution. You can reset the linked ID on the "Get Key" page, but be aware of the 7-day cooldown.

FREE Key Access:

If your account has **Free** status, you must complete the Linkvertise step once every 24 hours to generate a new key. The key will expire after 24 hours, requiring revalidation.
